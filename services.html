OUTREACH

ISKCON Coventry has been most fortunate to receive the mercy of numerous sannyasi’s and visiting spiritual leaders coming to Coventry. This invigorates and enthuses devotees resulting in active outreach events.

BAL GOPAL KIDS CLUB

The Bal Gopal Club was initiated in 2009 where children from the ages of 5 -12 years get together regularly and learn the vedic teachings, arts culture and history of ancient Bharat (India) & Krishna Conscious activities through drama, play, music, stories, writing, poetry, cooking, vaishnava etiquette and day summer trips. The teaching in shared between various devotees whom on a weekly basis take various activities with the children. If you would like your child to join in the Bal Gopal Club please contact us via email at kov@krishnaofvrindavan.com
